Famous card game, similar to whist.
The game can be played by up to 4 players. To win, one must achieve the number
of points chosen at the beginning of the game.

Controls:
Use keyboard to insert player names, the numbers of points one must achive to
win, etc.
Left and Right arrow keys or a and d to select a card or a bid.
Use ENTER to submit your choice.
q to quit the game.

CruceGame Usage:
cruceGame [-hv]
    -h, --help      Display this help
    -v, --version   Current Version of Cruce Game
