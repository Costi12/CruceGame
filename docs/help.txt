Cruce Game:
Famous card game, similar to whist.

CruceGame Usage:
cruceGame [-hv]
	-h, --help	Display help
	-v, --version Current Version of Cruce Game

No. of Players:
1-4

Goal:
Achieve the Game Points before your opponent.

Keyboard:
Left or Right arrow keys to choose the card.
a or s for card selection.
Use ENTER to place the card on table.
Standard keyboard to set other settings of game
like player name, game points.
q to quit the game.
