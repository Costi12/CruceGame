Cruce Game:
Famous card game, similar to whist.

CruceGame Usage:
cruceGame [-hv]

	-h, --help
		Display help

	-v, --version
		Current Version of Cruce Game

No. of Players:
1-4

Goal:
Achieve the Game Points before your opponent.
